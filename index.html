About

I am a third year Ph.D. candidate at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, advised by Dr. Chelsea Walton. My current research interests are Hopf algebras, Hopf monads and tensor categories. Other research interests include diagrammatic algebra and algebraic combinatorics.
